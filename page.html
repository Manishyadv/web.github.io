Driftwood Villas

Driftwood Villas is our latest project.

It's a contemporary style duplex villa and, thanks to its modularity, you can get your choice of 2, 3 or 4 bedroom configuration.

Its versatility make it ideal for either a stand-alone duplex on a small lot or connected townhouses: you can create a small or large gated community without having all the houses looking alike.

The square footage range from 1884 sq. ft. (175 sq. m.) for the 2 bedrooms up to 2035 sq, ft. (189 sq. m.) for the 4 bedrooms.
