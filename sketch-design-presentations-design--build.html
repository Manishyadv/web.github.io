Sketch Design

Have you purchased a piece of land and want to have an idea of what you can build on it?
If you ask an Architect for a proposal, it will usually be a fee proposal.
You can guess their style from their previous work but, until you commit, you won't be able to see if their ideas resonate with yours and if you're comfortable starting a professional relationship that will last for months or years.

Our Sketch Design service is perfect for you!
It's a stage before the usual Concept Design and not as costly.
After meeting with you and putting together a design brief, for a small fee, we'll provide schematic plans and perspectives showing you our ideas and proposals.
Our plans will show you the general distribution of your future house and our perspectives will illustrate our ideas on how your future house will look.
Our fee will be tailored to suit your needs, with black & white or water colored perspectives.

Presentations

This service usually completes our Sketch Design service, giving our client the full picture of their future house.
With the help of our partners in Italy (WIP Architetti), we can provide simple black & white sketches to convey our design ideas or fully water colored perspectives for a grand presentation to clients.

Design & Build

We already provide Design services for different companies.

If you're a builder and want to expand your services to your clients, Design & Build is what can make the difference.

More and more clients don't want to go through the hassle of having to deal with different individual professionals (builder, Architect, contractors, engineers, etc) during the construction of their home.
Providing them with Design & Build services can be a key service you're not providing yet.
We know that having an in house Architect full time may not be cost effective for your business.

M+ Architecture can provide this service for you.
We'll assist you with your client meetings and produce all the drawings you need to satisfy your client and feed your site.
Our fees will be tailored to your needs: as a builder, you probably already have in house some of the professionals you need to run a construction site smoothly and effectively.
We'll provide all (and only) the architectural services you need.
