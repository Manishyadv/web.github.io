Fix Cafe'

Located in Lanterns Mall in Hastings, Barbados, this modern and sophisticated cafe' is the perfect place to relax and enjoy a break sipping a locally roasted coffee with an awesome array of Italian pastry and gelato.
The cafe' opened in September 2016.
We kept an industrial feeling with the exposed ceiling painted black and the stainless steel suspended trunking to run all the services; all is warmed up through the use of natural materials: white ash wood counter tops, honey stained slat counter fronts, burlap and apple crates for the artwork, epoxy resin floors with a wood texture.
