Architectural & Design Services

Architecture

Our primary service is Architecture.
We'll assist our clients throughout the whole construction process, from Concept Design to completion.

Our extensive experience allows us to handle an extremely wide range of projects: from hotels and resorts to small gated communities, from luxury villas to cottages, from large scale urban planning to single units condos.
Budget and site constraints are taken into consideration to deliver the best design meeting our clients' needs and requirements.

Sketch Design, Presentations, Design & Build

Have you purchased a piece of land and want to get an idea of what you can build on it?
Don't want to spend too much or commit to one architect for the whole construction?
Our Concept Design service is perfect for you!
We'll provide schematic plans and perspective drawings showing you our ideas and proposals for a small fee.
If you're a builder and want to expand your service offering, we'll support you by providing a Design & Build service to your clients.

Interior Design

This is a service we usually include in our Architecture services as we like to provide the full package for maximum client satisfaction.
However, we do provide Interior Design as a separate service, such as in renovation projects for instance.

Furniture, Product  & Services Design

Hotels documentation

From Concept Design and Presentations to Construction Drawings, we coordinate with the whole team and meet the highest international  standards in drawings documentation.

Events & Stage Design
